# Game Overview

## 🎯 Vision Statement

**NecroDraft Arena** is a strategic top-down autobattler where players become necromancers, crafting unique undead minions from modular body parts. Each part carries special abilities that combine in powerful ways, creating deep tactical decisions in both drafting and assembly phases.

## 🎮 Core Experience

### What Makes It Unique
- **Modular Minion Crafting**: Unlike traditional autobattlers, units aren't fixed - they're assembled from interchangeable parts
- **Set Bonus Strategy**: Abilities require multiple parts of the same type, encouraging focused builds vs. versatility
- **Thematic Consistency**: Part themes (Skeleton, Zombie, Metal) have distinct identities and restrictions
- **Visual Progression**: Minions physically change as you equip different parts (future feature)

### Target Audience
- **Primary**: Strategy game enthusiasts who enjoy deck-building and autobattler mechanics
- **Secondary**: Players who like customization and progression systems
- **Age Range**: 13+ (dark fantasy theme but not graphic)

## 🔄 Core Game Loop

```
📋 Draft Phase
    ↓ Choose 1 of 3 random parts
🔧 Assembly Phase  
    ↓ Equip parts to minions for abilities
⚔️ Combat Phase
    ↓ Watch minions battle automatically
📊 Results Phase
    ↓ Victory = better rewards, Defeat = retry/adjust
🔄 Repeat → Stronger enemies, better parts
```

### Loop Duration
- **Draft**: 30-60 seconds (strategic decision)
- **Assembly**: 1-3 minutes (optimization puzzle)
- **Combat**: 30-90 seconds (tension and resolution)
- **Total per cycle**: 2-5 minutes

## 🎲 Risk vs. Reward

### Set Bonus Commitment
- **High Risk**: Dedicating multiple slots to one ability type
- **High Reward**: Powerful effects when threshold is reached
- **Strategic Depth**: Balance focused builds vs. diverse coverage

### Rarity Decisions
- **Common**: Safe, reliable power gains
- **Rare**: Good abilities with moderate risk
- **Epic**: Game-changing power with high opportunity cost

## 🌍 Setting & Theme

### World
- **Dark Fantasy**: Post-apocalyptic world where necromancy is commonplace
- **Arena Combat**: Gladiatorial entertainment for the undead masses
- **Modular Magic**: Death magic allows mixing and matching body parts

### Tone
- **Darkly Humorous**: Not grimdark - more Tim Burton than Game of Thrones
- **Strategic Focus**: Theme supports mechanics, not overwhelms them
- **Accessible**: Dark enough to be interesting, light enough to be fun

## 🎯 Success Metrics

### Prototype Goals
- **Engagement**: Players experiment with 5+ different part combinations
- **Strategic Depth**: Clear difference in power between random builds vs. optimized sets
- **Replayability**: Desire to try different approaches after first playthrough
- **Polish**: UI feels responsive and informative

### Long-term Vision
- **Content Depth**: 50+ unique parts across multiple themes
- **Visual Progression**: Full sprite layering system
- **Procedural Generation**: Infinite part variety through smart algorithms
- **Competitive Play**: Balanced enough for player vs. player modes

## 📐 Scope Boundaries

### What's In Scope
- **Core autobattler combat** with special abilities
- **Modular part system** with set bonuses
- **3-5 waves** of increasing difficulty
- **6-10 part types** across 2-3 themes
- **Polished UI** for drafting and assembly

### What's Out of Scope (For Now)
- **Real-time strategy** elements during combat
- **Story mode** or narrative campaigns
- **Multiplayer** functionality
- **Mobile platforms** (PC-first development)
- **3D graphics** or complex animations

## 🚀 Competitive Positioning

### Similar Games
- **Teamfight Tactics**: Autobattler mechanics, item combining
- **Hearthstone Battlegrounds**: Draft-based progression
- **Slay the Spire**: Strategic card selection, synergy building

### Key Differentiators
- **Physical customization**: Units visually change with parts
- **Set bonus thresholds**: Abilities require commitment, not just luck
- **Thematic restrictions**: Parts have lore-based limitations
- **Modular design**: Everything can be mixed and matched

## 📊 Platform & Technical

### Target Platform
- **Primary**: PC (Windows)
- **Engine**: Unity 6+ with 2D Core template
- **Distribution**: Itch.io for prototype, Steam for full release
- **Input**: Mouse and keyboard optimized

### Technical Goals
- **Performance**: 60 FPS with 10+ units on screen
- **Scalability**: System supports hundreds of part combinations
- **Modularity**: Easy to add new parts, abilities, and themes
- **Polish**: Smooth UI transitions and satisfying visual feedback
