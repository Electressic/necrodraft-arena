# Part Blueprints & Set Bonuses

## 🎯 Overview

This document defines the **complete part blueprint system** for NecroDraft Arena, including all class-specific parts, their stat pools, set bonuses, and the generic alternative parts.

## 🦴 Skeleton Set - Speed & Precision Theme

*"Fast, fragile, precise" - Glass cannon builds with hit-and-run tactics*

### Skeletal Skull (Head)
- **Primary Stats**: Crit Chance, Crit Damage, Range
- **Secondary Stats**: Attack Speed, Move Speed, Health
- **Set Bonus**: **Rattling Presence**
  - **Tier 1 (2 parts)**: Enemies attacking this minion have Attack Speed slowed by 10%
  - **Tier 2 (3 parts)**: Attack Speed slow increased to 15%
  - **Tier 3 (4 parts)**: At combat start, fears one random enemy for 1.5 seconds
- **Flavor**: *"Ancient cranium filled with supernatural dread"*

### Skeletal Ribcage (Torso)
- **Primary Stats**: Health, Defense, Dodge Chance
- **Secondary Stats**: Move Speed, Attack Speed
- **Set Bonus**: **Bone Armor**
  - **Tier 1 (2 parts)**: Gain a shield for 10% of max HP
  - **Tier 2 (3 parts)**: Shield increases to 15% of max HP
  - **Tier 3 (4 parts)**: Shield shatters on break, dealing AoE damage
- **Flavor**: *"Protective cage of calcified bone and dark magic"*

### Skeletal Arm Bones (Arms)
- **Primary Stats**: Attack, Attack Speed, Crit Chance
- **Secondary Stats**: Health, Range
- **Set Bonus**: **Brittle Bones**
  - **Tier 1 (2 parts)**: Attacks have 15% chance to apply "Brittle" (target takes +10% damage)
  - **Tier 2 (3 parts)**: Brittle's damage increase becomes 15%
  - **Tier 3 (4 parts)**: All attacks against a Brittle target have +20% Crit Chance
- **Flavor**: *"Sharpened bone weapons that weaken enemy defenses"*

### Skeletal Leg Bones (Legs)
- **Primary Stats**: Move Speed, Attack Speed, Dodge Chance
- **Secondary Stats**: Health, Crit Chance
- **Set Bonus**: **Bone Rush**
  - **Tier 1 (2 parts)**: After moving 5 tiles, next attack is a guaranteed crit
  - **Tier 2 (3 parts)**: Cooldown for this effect is reduced
  - **Tier 3 (4 parts)**: The critical hit also stuns the target for 1 second
- **Flavor**: *"Swift femurs that build momentum into devastating strikes"*

## 🧟 Zombie Set - Tank & Sustain Theme

*"Tanky, slow, sustaining" - Immovable tanks with sustained damage*

### Zombie Head (Head)
- **Primary Stats**: Defense, Health, Range  
- **Secondary Stats**: Attack Speed, Move Speed
- **Set Bonus**: **Rotten Stench**
  - **Tier 1 (2 parts)**: Creates a small aura that deals minor magic damage per second
  - **Tier 2 (3 parts)**: Aura damage is increased by 50%
  - **Tier 3 (4 parts)**: Aura applies 33% healing reduction to enemies inside it
- **Flavor**: *"Putrid skull emanating waves of toxic miasma"*

### Zombie Torso (Torso)
- **Primary Stats**: Health, Defense
- **Secondary Stats**: Attack, Move Speed
- **Set Bonus**: **Undying Hunger**
  - **Tier 1 (2 parts)**: Heals for 5% of max HP over 3 seconds after killing an enemy
  - **Tier 2 (3 parts)**: Healing increased to 8% of max HP
  - **Tier 3 (4 parts)**: If health drops below 20%, the healing effect triggers immediately
- **Flavor**: *"Rotting torso that feeds on the life force of enemies"*

### Zombie Arms (Arms)
- **Primary Stats**: Attack, Health
- **Secondary Stats**: Defense, Range
- **Set Bonus**: **Heavy Blows**
  - **Tier 1 (2 parts)**: Basic attacks have a 20% chance to stun for 0.5 seconds
  - **Tier 2 (3 parts)**: Stun chance increases to 25%
  - **Tier 3 (4 parts)**: The first attack against a new target is a guaranteed stun
- **Flavor**: *"Massive limbs that crush enemies with overwhelming force"*

### Zombie Legs (Legs)
- **Primary Stats**: Health, Move Speed, Defense
- **Secondary Stats**: Attack Speed, Range
- **Set Bonus**: **Dead Weight**
  - **Tier 1 (2 parts)**: Immune to knockback & slows
  - **Tier 2 (3 parts)**: Gains +15 Defense (special flat bonus)
  - **Tier 3 (4 parts)**: Enemies that attack this minion are slowed by 15%
- **Flavor**: *"Massive legs that anchor the undead like an unmovable fortress"*

## 👻 Ghost Set - Balanced & Magical Theme

*"Ethereal, magical, balanced" - Versatile builds with supernatural abilities*

### Ghostly Head (Head)
- **Primary Stats**: Range, Dodge Chance
- **Secondary Stats**: Attack Speed, Crit Chance
- **Set Bonus**: **Incorporeal**
  - **Tier 1 (2 parts)**: Takes 50% reduced damage from Critical Hits
  - **Tier 2 (3 parts)**: Gains +10% Dodge Chance
  - **Tier 3 (4 parts)**: When this minion dodges an attack, it gains +20% Attack Speed for its next attack
- **Flavor**: *"Ethereal skull that phases between material and spirit realms"*

### Ghostly Torso (Torso)
- **Primary Stats**: Dodge Chance, Health
- **Secondary Stats**: Move Speed, Attack Speed
- **Set Bonus**: **Phase Shift**
  - **Tier 1 (2 parts)**: Every 5 seconds, the minion becomes untargetable for 0.75 seconds
  - **Tier 2 (3 parts)**: Untargetable duration increases to 1 second
  - **Tier 3 (4 parts)**: When phasing back in, the next attack deals bonus magic damage
- **Flavor**: *"Spectral core that flickers between dimensions"*

### Ghostly Arms (Arms)
- **Primary Stats**: Attack Speed, Range, Attack
- **Secondary Stats**: Health, Crit Damage
- **Set Bonus**: **Ethereal Touch**
  - **Tier 1 (2 parts)**: Attacks pass through the target, hitting an enemy behind them for 25% damage
  - **Tier 2 (3 parts)**: Cleave damage increased to 33%
  - **Tier 3 (4 parts)**: Main target also has their Defense reduced by 15%
- **Flavor**: *"Spectral limbs that reach through physical matter"*

### Ghostly Legs (Legs)
- **Primary Stats**: Move Speed, Dodge Chance, Attack Speed
- **Secondary Stats**: Health, Range
- **Set Bonus**: **Spectral Step**
  - **Tier 1 (2 parts)**: This minion can move through other units
  - **Tier 2 (3 parts)**: Gains +15% Movement Speed
  - **Tier 3 (4 parts)**: After moving through an enemy, that enemy is slowed by 20%
- **Flavor**: *"Ethereal legs that glide effortlessly through the battlefield"*

## 🏗️ Build Archetypes

### Pure Set Builds (4 matching parts)

#### Full Skeleton Build
- **Power**: Maximum Bone Rush + Bone Armor + Brittle Bones + Rattling Presence
- **Strengths**: Ultimate glass cannon with defensive utility
- **Weaknesses**: Lower total HP, vulnerable to burst damage
- **Playstyle**: Hit-and-run, kiting, precision strikes

#### Full Zombie Build  
- **Power**: Maximum tank synergies across all slots
- **Strengths**: Nearly indestructible, self-sustaining
- **Weaknesses**: Low mobility, can be outmaneuvered
- **Playstyle**: Frontline tank, attrition warfare

#### Full Ghost Build
- **Power**: Ultimate versatility and phase mechanics
- **Strengths**: Adaptive to any situation, hard to pin down
- **Weaknesses**: No single overwhelming strength
- **Playstyle**: Flexible flanker, magic damage dealer

### Hybrid Builds (2+2 sets)

#### Skeleton/Ghost Hybrid (2+2)
- **Power**: Tier 1 Bone Rush + Tier 1 Phase Shift
- **Strengths**: Speed + survivability, balanced offense
- **Weaknesses**: No Tier 3 bonuses, moderate power
- **Playstyle**: Mobile striker with escape options

#### Zombie/Generic Hybrid (2+2)
- **Power**: Tier 1 Zombie set + higher stat budget from generics
- **Strengths**: Tank synergy + superior stats
- **Weaknesses**: Less specialized than pure builds
- **Playstyle**: Stat-focused tank with set utility

### Stat-Focused Builds (Generic emphasis)

#### Pure Generic Build
- **Power**: Maximum stat budget across all slots (+25% each)
- **Strengths**: Superior raw stats, flexible adaptation
- **Weaknesses**: No set bonuses, purely stat-dependent
- **Playstyle**: Overwhelming stats, straightforward combat

## 🎯 Strategic Implications

### Draft Considerations
- **Early Game**: Focus on stat accumulation and basic synergies
- **Mid Game**: Commit to 2-piece set strategies for reliable power
- **Late Game**: Choose between 4-piece specialization or hybrid flexibility

### Counter-Strategies
- **Anti-Skeleton**: High HP builds to survive burst, AoE to handle mobility
- **Anti-Zombie**: High sustained DPS, kiting, burst damage
- **Anti-Ghost**: Consistent damage to bypass dodge, AoE abilities
- **Anti-Generic**: Set synergy builds to match their stat advantage

### Meta Balance
- **Rock-Paper-Scissors**: Each pure build counters one and loses to another
- **Hybrid Viability**: 2+2 builds provide consistency against all strategies
- **Generic Niche**: Stat-focused builds for players who prioritize raw power

This blueprint system ensures every part has clear identity while maintaining strategic depth through the interplay of themes, rarities, and set bonuses.
