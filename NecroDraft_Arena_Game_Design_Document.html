🎯 Vision Statement

NecroDraft Arena is a strategic top-down autobattler where players become necromancers, crafting unique undead minions from modular body parts. Each part carries special abilities that combine in powerful ways, creating deep tactical decisions in both drafting and assembly phases.

🎮 What Makes It Unique

  • Modular Minion Crafting: Unlike traditional autobattlers, units aren't fixed - they're assembled from interchangeable parts
  • Set Bonus Strategy: Abilities require multiple parts of the same type, encouraging focused builds vs. versatility
  • Thematic Consistency: Part themes (Skeleton, Zombie, Metal) have distinct identities and restrictions
  • Visual Progression: Minions physically change as you equip different parts (future feature)

🔄 Core Game Loop

📋 Draft Phase ↓ Choose 1 of 3 random parts 🔧 Assembly Phase ↓ Equip parts to minions for abilities ⚔️ Combat Phase ↓ Watch minions battle automatically 📊 Results Phase ↓ Victory = better rewards, Defeat = retry/adjust 🔄 Repeat → Stronger enemies, better parts

🎮 Core Mechanics

🧩 Part-Based Minion System

Every minion has 4 equipment slots that define their capabilities:

  • Head: Often provides mental/precision abilities (CriticalStrike, etc.)
  • Torso: Often provides defensive/sustain abilities (Armored, Regeneration)
  • Arms: Often provides offensive abilities (Vampiric, Thorns)
  • Legs: Often provides mobility abilities (Swift, etc.)

📊 Stat Calculation

Final HP = Base HP + Head HP + Torso HP + Arms HP + Legs HP
Final ATK = Base ATK + Head ATK + Torso ATK + Arms ATK + Legs ATK
Final Speed = Base Speed × Speed Multipliers (from abilities)

⚔️ Set Bonus Strategy

Key Mechanic: Abilities only activate with 2 or more parts of the same type.

1 Vampiric Part   → Only base stats (HP/ATK bonus)
2 Vampiric Parts  → Ability activates at Tier 1
3 Vampiric Parts  → Ability activates at Tier 2  
4 Vampiric Parts  → Ability activates at Tier 3 (maximum power)

🎲 Draft Strategy

Players must choose 1 of 3 random parts each turn, creating meaningful decisions:

Safe Strategy

Pick highest rarity regardless of synergy

Greedy Strategy

Commit early to specific set bonuses

Flexible Strategy

Adapt based on what's offered

Counter Strategy

Draft to counter expected enemy types

⚔️ Combat System

Combat is fully automated - players influence through preparation, not execution:

  • Unit AI: Simple targeting and movement rules
  • Positioning: Units spawn in formation, move to engage
  • Turn Order: Based on speed, modified by abilities
  • Victory Condition: Last army standing wins

🧩 Part System

🎨 Theme Characteristics

💀 Skeleton Parts

Identity: Precise, brittle, bone-based undead

Stat Profile: Moderate HP, High ATK

Signature Abilities: CriticalStrike, Swift

Restrictions: ❌ Cannot have Armored, Regeneration, or Vampiric

🧟 Zombie Parts

Identity: Decaying, resilient, flesh-based undead

Stat Profile: High HP, Moderate ATK

Signature Abilities: Vampiric, Poison, Regeneration

Restrictions: ❌ Cannot have Swift or CriticalStrike

🤖 Metal Parts

Identity: Mechanical, armored, artificial constructs

Stat Profile: Very High HP, Low ATK

Signature Abilities: Armored, Thorns

Restrictions: ❌ Cannot have Poison, Vampiric, or Regeneration

🌟 Cursed Parts

Identity: Dark magic, high risk/reward, forbidden arts

Stat Profile: Extreme stats (very high or very low)

Signature Abilities: Berserker, unique cursed effects

Special Rules: ⚠️ May have negative effects alongside positives

💎 Set Bonuses & Abilities

Tier 1 - Activation at 2 Parts

Vampiric
  • 2 Parts: Heal for 25% of damage dealt
  • 3 Parts: Heal for 35% of damage dealt
  • 4 Parts: Heal for 50% of damage dealt

Strategy: Sustain fighter, good against chip damage

Armored
  • 2 Parts: Reduce incoming damage by 1 (minimum 1)
  • 3 Parts: Reduce incoming damage by 2 (minimum 1)
  • 4 Parts: Reduce incoming damage by 3 (minimum 1)

Strategy: Tank, counters low-damage rapid attacks

Critical Strike
  • 2 Parts: 25% chance for double damage
  • 3 Parts: 35% chance for double damage
  • 4 Parts: 25% chance for triple damage

Strategy: Burst damage, high variance

Tier 2 - Activation at 3 Parts

Swift
  • 3 Parts: +100% move speed, -25% HP
  • 4 Parts: +150% move speed, -20% HP (reduced penalty)

Strategy: Hit and run, flanking maneuvers

Berserker
  • 3 Parts: +50% attack speed when below 50% HP
  • 4 Parts: +75% attack speed when below 60% HP

Strategy: Comeback mechanic, high risk/reward

Scalable System
